# Sacramento Mobile EV Charging

Emergency roadside EV charging service for Folsom, CA and the greater Sacramento region.

---

## Overview

When an electric vehicle runs out of charge in Folsom, CA, waiting for a tow truck or searching for a charging station is not always an option. Sacramento Mobile EV Charging provides 24/7 emergency roadside EV charging designed to get stranded drivers moving again quickly and safely.

This service is built specifically for real-world EV emergencies, including miscalculated range, unexpected detours, charging station failures, and dead batteries in inconvenient locations.

---

## Service Area

**Primary Coverage:** Folsom, CA  
**Secondary Coverage:** El Dorado Hills, Rancho Cordova, Orangevale, Fair Oaks, Roseville, Granite Bay, Citrus Heights, Sacramento

Sacramento Mobile EV Charging responds to emergency EV charging calls throughout the greater Sacramento region.

---

## Common Emergency Scenarios

### Shopping Centers and Parking Lots
Drivers stranded at locations such as Folsom Premium Outlets or grocery store parking lots with insufficient charge to reach the next station.

### Highway Emergencies
Out-of-charge situations on Highway 50 near East Bidwell or Folsom Blvd exits, requiring safe roadside charging coordination.

### Residential Areas
Dead EV batteries in neighborhoods such as Empire Ranch, Broadstone, and Palladio after visiting family or completing errands.

---

## How the Service Works

1. Contact Sacramento Mobile EV Charging  
2. Provide your location (street, parking lot, or highway exit)  
3. A mobile charging unit is dispatched  
4. On-site charging is provided to reach the next public charger or destination
